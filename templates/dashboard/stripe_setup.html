How to connect Stripe

  1. 1
    Create a free Stripe account

    If you don't have one yet, go to stripe.com/register and sign up. It takes about 2 minutes. You'll need your business name, email, and bank account details to start accepting real payments.

    Stripe is free to create. They charge a small fee per successful payment (see pricing). There are no monthly fees.
  2. 2
    Go to your API keys page

    In your Stripe dashboard, click Developers → API keys in the left sidebar. Or use this direct link:

    Open Stripe API Keys
  3. 3
    Copy your two keys

    You'll see two keys on that page:

    pk_live_… Publishable key — safe to show publicly. Starts with pk_live_ (or pk_test_ for testing).
    sk_live_… Secret key — keep this private. Click "Reveal live key" to see it. Starts with sk_live_.
    Never share your secret key — treat it like a password. We store it securely and never display it in full after you save.
  4. 4
    Paste both keys here and save

    Paste them into the form on the right and click Save & Verify. We'll confirm the connection with Stripe before saving.

Test keys vs. Live keys

Stripe gives you two sets of keys: test (for trying things out — no real money moves) and live (for real payments from real customers).

While building your store, use test keys. Switch to live keys when you're ready to go live. Test card numbers: 4242 4242 4242 4242, any future date, any 3-digit CVC.
